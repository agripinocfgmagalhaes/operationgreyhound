Operation Greyhound

Who we are

Operation Greyhound is a non-profit all-volunteer organization located in San Diego, California, and dedicated to the placement of retired racing greyhounds in loving homes. We are a non-profit, tax-exempt 501(c)(3) organization and are completely dependent on the donations from generous souls and the work of our volunteers to help us to save precious greyhounds.

What we do

Our mission is to find loving homes for retired greyhounds. So far, we have placed over 3,000 greyhounds in Southern California. Our facilities will accommodate 21 greyhounds and we estimate that more than 20,000 greyhounds need to be rescued annually from U.S. greyhound race tracks. These dogs are gentle and loving and they thrive on human companionship.

How to help

There are many ways to help Operation Greyhound. Donations are always appreciated and we take cash, credit cards, and checks and you can designate that your contributions go to a special-needs greyhound in our “sponsor” program. We also accept items like kennel equipment and dog food. And if you’d like to volunteer, we need everything from office work to kennel help.
